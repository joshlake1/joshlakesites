Our Philosophy

Meticulous sourcing. Intentional roasting.

We believe the morning ritual shouldn't be rushed. It’s a moment to center yourself before the day unfolds.

Every single bean we grind is ethically sourced directly from small-holder farms in Ethiopia and Colombia. Roasted micro-batches weekly ensure that what hits your cup is vibrant, clean, and perfectly balanced.
