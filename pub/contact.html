By Train: Ashford International Station is a 10-minute walk. Exit via the town centre and head north on High Street.

By Car: Free parking after 6pm on High Street. NCP car park on Elwick Road (5-minute walk). Sat nav: TN24 8TD.

By Bus: Routes 1, 2, and 42 stop directly outside on High Street.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book for Sunday roast?

Yes, strongly recommended. We frequently sell out by Friday. Book online or call us to secure your table.

Are children welcome?

Absolutely. We have high chairs, a children's menu, and a welcoming atmosphere for families. Children are welcome until 9pm.

Do you accommodate dietary requirements?

Yes. We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please mention any allergies when booking and inform your server on arrival.

Is the pub dog-friendly?

Dogs are welcome in the bar area and beer garden. They're not permitted in the dining room, but water bowls are always available at the bar.

Do you have wheelchair access?

Yes. We have step-free access via our side entrance on Church Passage, and an accessible toilet on the ground floor.
