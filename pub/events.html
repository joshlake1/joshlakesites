What's On

Events & Entertainment

From live music to quiz nights, there's always something happening at The Copper Kettle.

Weekly

Regular Events

Mark your calendar — these events run every week and are always free to attend.

Wednesday Quiz Night

Helen's legendary pub quiz — eight rounds of general knowledge, picture rounds, and a music round. £2 entry per person, teams of up to six. Prizes for the top three teams plus a booby prize nobody wants.

Every Wednesday · 8pm

Friday Live Music

Local and touring acoustic acts take to our intimate stage every Friday evening. From folk to blues to singer-songwriters — always free entry, always brilliant atmosphere.

Every Friday · 8:30pm

Sunday Roast

Our legendary Sunday roasts are an institution. Four choices of meat (or nut roast), unlimited gravy, and all the trimmings. Booking essential — we sell out every week.

Every Sunday · 12pm – 6pm
Coming Up

Upcoming Special Events

One-off events and seasonal celebrations you won't want to miss.

04 Jul

Summer Beer Festival

Friday 4th – Sunday 6th July · 12pm – 11pm daily

Our annual beer festival returns with over 20 guest ales from Kent microbreweries, live music all weekend, and street food vendors in our beer garden. Free entry, pay-as-you-go.

12 Jul

Jazz & Gin Night

Saturday 12th July · 7:30pm – 11pm

The Ashford Jazz Quartet performs an evening of smooth standards. Special gin tasting menu featuring five Kent distilleries. Tickets £15 including two gin tastings.

26 Jul

Open Mic Night

Saturday 26th July · 8pm onwards

Got talent? Our monthly open mic welcomes musicians, poets, and comedians. Sign up from 7:30pm at the bar. Free entry for performers and audience alike.

09 Aug

Kentish Wine Evening

Saturday 9th August · 7pm – 10pm

An evening celebrating Kent's flourishing wine scene. Six wines paired with canapés from our kitchen. Hosted by Sophie, our bar manager and qualified sommelier. Tickets £25.

31 Oct

Halloween Murder Mystery Dinner

Friday 31st October · 7pm – 11pm

A three-course dinner with a side of suspense. Interactive murder mystery set in our Victorian snugs. Fancy dress encouraged. Tickets £45 per person, limited to 40 guests.

Celebrate With Us

Private Hire & Functions

Whether you're planning a birthday celebration, a wake, a corporate dinner, or a wedding reception, The Copper Kettle offers flexible private hire options to suit every occasion.

The Snug — Intimate space for 15–25 guests. Perfect for birthday dinners and small gatherings.

The Main Bar — Exclusive hire for 50–80 guests. Ideal for larger celebrations with full bar service.

The Beer Garden — Outdoor space for up to 100 guests (April–September). Marquee available for weather cover.

All packages include bespoke menus, dedicated staff, and flexible bar options. We can accommodate dietary requirements and themed events.
