Our Story

Oak & Bean Café is a small independent coffee shop located near the heart of Romford. We opened in 2023 with a simple goal — to serve high-quality coffee, fresh food, and create a relaxed space for the local community. Everything is prepared fresh daily, from our hand-baked pastries to our locally sourced sandwiches. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso on the go or sitting in for a slow brunch, we aim to make you feel at home.
