About Us

BrightSpark Electrical Solutions is a family-owned electrical contractor serving residential and commercial customers across the Greater Manchester area since 2014. With over a decade of experience, our certified electricians specialize in installations, repairs, maintenance, and emergency callouts.

Our mission is simple: provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services while delivering exceptional customer care.
